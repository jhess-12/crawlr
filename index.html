About

Crawlr is a 21+ social hub, spotlighting local restuarants and bars, their events, and what your community has to say. Simply browse your favorite spots in your city, see whats happening, how the lines and ride rates are and start crawling.

Features

Real Time Reports:

  • Locals on Crawlr can report how long bar lines are, ride rates and more.

Events:

  • Crawlr displays happy hours, live music, bar crawls, and more that restuarants and bars in a community are offering.

Social Feed:

  • Users can post their crawl, sharing their expeirance to their community.
